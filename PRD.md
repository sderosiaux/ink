# Vision

Create my own publishing engine where I write once and the system handles everything: drafting, rewriting, visuals, scheduling, publishing, and cross-channel packaging.
I want a place that fits my style, speeds me up, and runs on automation.
This platform becomes my home for Medium-level essays, Substack newsletters, daily posts, HN analyses, and anything I want to share.

The experience feels like:
I drop an idea.
The system turns it into content.
I pick what I like.
I publish or schedule.

---

# High Level Product Requirements

## 1. Content Hub

Store every idea and draft in one place.
I can create, tag, search, and shape pieces without friction.

**Requirements**

* Create new draft
* Save notes, ideas, audio, links
* Tag system
* Search across all content
* Import older Medium/Substack posts

---

## 2. Writing Engine

A set of prompts and agents that can turn any input into a draft or full article.
The system reflects my voice.

**Requirements**

* Generate first draft from text, URL, transcript, HN thread
* Rewrite, shorten, expand, reorganize
* Create multiple variants
* Title generator
* Social versions (LinkedIn, X)

---

## 3. Image Engine

Automatic visual generation from Gemini Banana Pro.
I only choose which one I want.

* Auto-suggest images based on article content
* Store images with metadata

---

## 4. Backoffice Review Space

A simple board where I see everything that is ready, missing something, or almost finished.
I can shape the piece before it goes live.

**Requirements**

* Kanban style: Ideas, Drafts, Ready, Scheduled
* Preview article
* Swap images
* Compare versions
* Pick the final version

---

## 5. Scheduling and Publishing

Full control over when things go live.
One click to send to my blog, Medium, Substack, LinkedIn, or X.

**Requirements**

* Publish to my blog
* Cross-post to Medium/Substack
* Schedule at date and time
* Preview social cards
* Auto-generate LinkedIn posts at the same time

---

## 6. Automation Layer

Rules that run in the background and create content or suggestions on their own.

**Requirements**

* Night mode that produces new drafts from notes
* Auto-draft when a saved link hits HN or Reddit
* Auto-expand tagged ideas
* Auto-suggest follow-up content
* Auto-check tone alignment with my preferred voice

---

## 7. Versioning

Everything I write or generate stays traceable.
I can always go back.

**Requirements**

* Version history
* Compare two versions
* Rollback

---

## 8. Blog Frontend

A clean site that loads fast and highlights my writing.

**Requirements**

* Modern layout
* Strong SEO basics
* Clean typography
* Tag pages
* Search bar
* Fast rendering

---

## 9. Manual Override

Even with automation everywhere, I stay in control.

**Requirements**

* Approve before publishing
* Edit manual drafts
* Select images
* Disable auto-generation per article

---

# The North Star

A personal publishing engine that works like a private newsroom.
I write ideas. The system turns them into final pieces.
I stay in control of taste.
Everything else becomes automatic.
